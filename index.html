About Armour Carrier

Armour Carrier LLC specializes in hauling a variety of freight.

Or equipment on a flatbed or lowboy trailer. Based in Kerrville, Texas, Armour Carrier operates in the state of Texas or long-haul routes in the continental U.S.

Armour Carrier is a fully licensed, insured and DOT compliant firm that hires only U.S. citizens that have fluent English-speaking skills. Armour Carrier operates under MC#1476302 and USDOT# 3992432.

We pride ourselves on hiring highly experienced professional drivers with excellent driving records that operate the newest fleet of equipment. We take pride in our on-time pickup and delivery performance as well as safely delivering our customer’s freight.

Our Services

Armour Carrier can haul a wide range of regulation size and oversized freight. Our team of experts will come up with a plan that is safe and efficient to get your freight to its destination. No matter how big or small the haul is, safety is our number one priority.

  • Flatbed Hauling

    We utilize 53’ foot flatbed trailers. Freight can be tarped if required and we can accommodate coils as well.

  • Lowboy Hauling

    Our Lowboy has a 25’ foot well that can be utilized for hauling equipment and other job materials.

  • Oversized Hauling

    Armour Carrier LLC has the proper equipment to handle the oversized and overweight loads.

Our Mission

ArmourCarrier is immensely grateful for all the sacrifices and demanding work from our active military and veterans.

We show our support by donating a percentage of our proceeds to several different organizations. Our mission is to encourage other individuals to support an outstanding organization and to support businesses that are directly involved with the Americans Mighty Warriors.

This foundation offers a helping hand to soldiers who have been injured in the line of duty by fundraising, spreading awareness, and empowering the recipients. We would also like to mention the generous work done by Fontaine Trailer and their collaboration with America’s Mighty Warriors.

Fontaine Trailer donates $1,000 of each sale of their Patriot Edition Trailers to America’s Mighty Warriors

This organization funds medical treatments for veterans suffering from PTSD and TBI. All of us here at Armour Carrier are proud to support such a worthy cause.

The Fontaine Trailer that Armour Carrier purchased honors the life of Navy Seal Marc Alan Lee. He was the first SEAL to lose his life in Operation Iraqi Freedom when he was killed in a fierce firefight while on patrol against insurgents in Ramadi. He was posthumously awarded the Silver Star, Bronze Star with Valor, and the Purple Heart. May God bless his family and we salute him for his service.

Other Organizations that Armour Carrier supports are the Wounded Warrier Project and Veterans Association of America.
